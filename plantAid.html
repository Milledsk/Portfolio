PlantAid

An ambient display to teach young adults to recognize house plants visual cues.

Prototype

Koncept

By creating an artificial plant to mimic and visualise the physical clues of actual household plants, young adults can learn the cues and take care of their plants.

Prototype

The prototype consists of an artificial plant and a sensor in the real plant. The artificial plants show cues such as bending and turning leefs brown, when the real plants sensor information shows that the plant needs attention.

Technologies

The prototype is developed with two microcontrollers communicating with xbees. The artificial plant has a servo and leds to show cues, and the sensor in the real plant has a temperature sensor and a moist sensor.

Technologies

Microcontroller, xbee, sensors and actuators
